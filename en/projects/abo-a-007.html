  • Description

    This 'FuelCalculator' app provides the necessary formula to calculate the amount of fuel for a given trip, or maybe to calculate the total price you need to pay for fuel to travel some distance, or simply calculate the distance you can travel with the fuel amount purchased. There are many more combinations of known data and needed data to calculate. Fuel economy, distance, fuel amount and payment amount can be calculated using the 'FuelCalculator' app. It works for any kind of vehicles: cars, vans, buses, motorcycles, trucks etc. Simply download the app and try the features it offers.
