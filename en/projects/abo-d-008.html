  • Description

    'StaroNovo' is a platform for selling and buying clothes, either second-hand or manufactured in your own workshop. Everyone can login/register and use this simple and modern application, and everyone could be either a seller or a buyer. This is the initial design of the application. This application design was made using the Figma tool. You can find more information about the whole project when exploring the platform (see link above).
