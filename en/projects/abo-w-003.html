  • Description

    'FINKI eSystem' is a web system needed for universities and faculties. With this web app professors can have control over their students, their grades and their success. They can also have control over the subjects they teach and have a better organization of the semester. An admin can perform changes to the subject list, the professor list and also the availability of the exam session list.
