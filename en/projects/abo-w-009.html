  • Description

    'Gesundheitsförderung Schweiz' is legally mandated to initiate, coordinate and evaluate measures to promote health and prevent diseases. They support the diverse efforts of those involved in health promotion and prevention so that as many people and organizations as possible behave healthily and create a health-promoting environment. They provide Cantonal Action Programs, Prevention in Health Care, Corporate Health Management and Campaigns. You can find more details on the links above.
