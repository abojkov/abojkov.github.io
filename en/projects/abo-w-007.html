  • Description

    'Busko' is a modern and interactive web application that was created as part of the e-Commerce course that I took during my university education. It is a user-based system consisting of different types of roles, including user, manager, employee, admin etc. This web app gives you the opportunity to reserve bus tickets from the comfort of your home, workplace or simply wherever you are. This trip search web app is available for transport in North Macedonia only.
