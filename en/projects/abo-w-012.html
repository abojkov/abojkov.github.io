    Papershift App - All-in-one staff rota, timesheet, absence & payroll software. Pulse is next generation child application for HR employee management. Onboardings, employee tasks, recruiting and many more processes made easy with Pulse. Pulse is still in its implementation phase, so be ready for this Papershift glow-up application soon. Every change and feature implemented is directly available on the app, so you can also follow the implementation process from a user perspective daily.
