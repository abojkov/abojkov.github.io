    As a part of the Netcetera family, Pulse.eco was the project assigned to me. Pulse.eco is a crowdsourcing platform, which gathers and presents environmental data. Our network of sensor installations and other third-party sources gathers the data and translates them into visual and easy to understand information. My team and I currently develop this Android Application and make it better every day. For more information about the project, visit the link above.
