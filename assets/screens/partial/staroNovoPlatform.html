About this platform Expand  

'StaroNovo' is a platform used to a) promote and sell, and b) discover and buy different kind of apparel and clothing. This intro sentence obviously defines two types of users - sellers and buyers. Everyone can use this platform (web app or mobile app) and every user can be a seller and a buyer at the same time. This platform simply connects those who want to sell and those who want to buy stuff. When a connection is established, i.e. when a buyer orders some products from the seller, the seller needs to externally arrange transportation of these products to the buyer. This is not part of the 'StaroNovo' platform. Externally, 'myCargo' platform can be used for this. Learn more about 'myCargo' in the portfolio section.
